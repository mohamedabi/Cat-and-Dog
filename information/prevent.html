How to prevent animal abuse?

Reporting a case:
• a precise description of the situation and the circumstances under which the maltreatment occurred
• the name and address of the perpetrator - whether the owner of the animal or the alien
• when and where the abuse was committed and whether it is a long-term or accidental abuse
• describe whether the animal shows signs of physical or psychological abuse, signs of malnutrition
• contact with you - if you ask for it, your personal information will remain secret!
• Few people know that the obligation to report the case of animal abuse to the police or the RVPS imposes the law on every citizen

In order to make tampering easier and you are witnessing it, it is best to capture the beast - the injuries, the conditions in which it lives, the overall and detailed view of the animal, and other shots that might help solve the problem. It is advisable to have witnesses (at least one). Of course, it is essential to report every case of abuse, even if you do not have all of this information or photographic documentation.

By law, the staff of the shelter can not arbitrarily enter the land of the pet owner and take it out of bad conditions, for which it is the competent authority of the RVPS.

RVPS - Regional Veterinary and Food Administration, which may, after investigating the case, impose remedies or prohibit rearing. A private individual may impose a fine of up to EUR 300 and a legal entity raising up to EUR 16,500. According to the Criminal Code, it is possible to impose a custodial sentence of 2 years on the animal abuses.

How can you handle your case?

In case of suspicion of animal abuse, you can contact the Inspector of the Regional Veterinary Administration - RVPS, who is obliged to deal with any complaint under Act No. 152/1998 on violation of the Act on Veterinary Care and Animal Protection no. 488/2002. The complaint shall be filed in writing, stating the information referred to in the preceding point. If you decide to send an initiative yourself, do not forget to ask RVPS to inform you of the progress and outcome of the solution.

You can also:

- report the case to the closest shelter in the neighborhood,
- the mayor of the city - the village Note: The more reports pertaining to the particular case are submitted to the RVPS, the better!
