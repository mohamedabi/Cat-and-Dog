ANNUAL REPORT 2011


PRESIDENCY OF THE PRESIDENT

The next year of our organization is successful behind us, thanks to the willingness of many volunteers and hearts, contributors, sponsors and supporters. Last year, we overwhelmed many changes, some managed self-help, others learned, and many people helped us, who we did not even expect. This assures us that our not easy work is meaningful and people perceive us as a protector. In today's difficult times, we appreciate the help of those who indulge and sacrifice to help continue to be the most joyous, and we hope that this year we and our help will be able to meet our plans to further improve the conditions and dignity of abandoned animals.
