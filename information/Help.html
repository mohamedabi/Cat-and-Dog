How to help us


Animals really need to help us all. Without public support, organizations like our Animal Welfare are not able to work. So help! Even if you do not feel material rich, you surely choose to support animal welfare:

  • Contribute with an eternal gift
  • Contribute financially
  • Contribute a free service
  • Activist

At home or at work, you can find things you no longer need or you can surrender. Give us them! We need in particular: pet food, leashes, collars, detergents and disinfectants, medicines for animals, brooms, shovels, buckets, clothes, building materials to complete the shelter, work clothes, gloves, office supplies - pens, envelopes, paper, markers, colors ...

Your gift will be received with great thanks. The money will be used for the operation and operation of the shelter of the Regional Center of Animal Freedom Piešťany. You can contribute by postal money order to our Post Office account: 20046882/6500 or directly to: Regional Center Freedom Animal Piešťany, PO BOX D 111, 92101 Piešťany. Donations We can donate in person, or you can contribute to a particular thing (construction pen, treatment of a particular dog, ...) can also contribute in the framework of donating 2% of income tax (currently form the download is usually placed on the main page during the transfer tax returns).

If you are a business owner or an expert on any of the following or other areas, use it to provide free work to the benefit of the animals in the shelter!

We need help in:
  • Construction work on the completion of the shelter
  • Carpentry work
  • Welding work
  • Gardening work
  • Graphic works and printing of information materials Car repair

If you are from Piestany, or the surrounding area - become an activist, that is a man who in his spare time voluntarily and unselfishly helps in all areas of the organization - which represents, for example, help in a shelter - walking the dogs, help with events, finding sponsors, inspections adopted dogs, temporary care of sick animals and puppies in Piešťany, lectures at elementary school ... We are already looking forward to our joint work on animal rescue!
